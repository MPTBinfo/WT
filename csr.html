Preserve Culture, Empower Livelihoods, and Champion Sustainability

This CSR initiative aims to replace outdated, polluting engines in traditional boats with modern eco-friendly Yamaha engines ‚Äî empowering boatmen, preserving Maheshwar's heritage, and promoting clean river tourism.

About the Region ‚Äì Maheshwar, MP

Maheshwar is an ancient town on the Narmada River known for its temples, forts, ghats, and traditional handloom sarees. It is a focal point of spiritual tourism in Madhya Pradesh, drawing thousands of domestic and international visitors every year. River boating is not just a tourist activity here ‚Äî it is a lifeline for over 75 families from the local Kewat and Mallah communities.

Problem Statement

Most of the boats in use rely on antiquated 2-stroke diesel engines. These are not only highly polluting and fuel-inefficient but also compromise passenger safety and increase maintenance burdens. The noise disrupts the peaceful spiritual ambiance of the Narmada riverfront and creates a negative experience for tourists.

Our Vision

The project envisions replacing traditional engines with Yamaha 9.9 HP 4-stroke engines ‚Äî which are safer, quieter, more fuel-efficient, and environmentally friendly. Each upgraded boat will also receive new signage, basic refurbishing, and operator training.
